ärthron C/T WRAP™
ärthron's C/T Wrap™ helps trainers provide therapy to athletes following hamstring pulls and thigh bruises. The C/T Wrap innovatively relies on patented C/T Pak that delivers both hot AND cold therapy. The Pak can either be frozen or microwaved, then inserted in the Wrap, where it retains its temperature longer than traditional hot or cold packs. For thigh bruises, the C/T Wrap rotates to the front of the leg, which is kept in flexion by a strap. The adjustable Wrap fits almost everyone and is easy for athletes to use at home or in a dorm room.
