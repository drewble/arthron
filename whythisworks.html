54 Degrees of Protection
There is an optimum angle.
The Dome provides a protective shield that deflects forces away from the injured area.

ärthron's IMPACT™ Pads deflect blows away from a protected area at a 54-degree angle. Why 54? Because extensive studies show that 54 degrees is the optimum angle for intercepting and dispersing a potentially damaging blow.

As a result, athletes wearing our IMPACT Pads can play safely, with a greater confidence and without fear of reinjury. That confidence can be a key to peak athletic performance.

In our case, 54 degrees is a right angle.
